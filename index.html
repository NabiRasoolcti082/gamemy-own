Welcome to GTA

Grand Theft Auto is an action-adventure game series developed by Rockstar Games. In the game, players assume the role of a criminal and navigate through the city, completing missions and engaging in various criminal activities.

Gameplay

GTA offers an open-world environment where players can explore and interact with the game world. The game features a variety of vehicles, weapons, and tools that players can use to complete missions and progress through the game.
